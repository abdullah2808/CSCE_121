chuchuyimlang is a village in mokokchung district in the state of nagaland in northeast india. the village is divided into four sectors or "mopu", namely longzung mopu, teyong mopu, impang mopu and imlang mopu. the "compound area" comes under different constituency though it is also a part of chuchuyimlang village, it is also referred to as "chuchu town".
